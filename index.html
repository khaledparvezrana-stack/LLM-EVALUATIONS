Common Pitfalls
🌊
Benchmark Saturation

When top models score near the ceiling, the benchmark stops separating good from great. Always use recent, harder benchmarks.

🧪
Data Contamination

The model may have seen test data during training, inflating benchmark scores. Prefer contamination-resistant datasets.

⚖️
Judge Bias (LLM)

Position bias, verbosity bias, and self-preference bias can skew LLM-as-a-Judge scores. Always validate against human ratings.

📉
Metric Drift

Model behavior can silently change after fine-tuning or provider updates. Continuous production monitoring is non-negotiable.
